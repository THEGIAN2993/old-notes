\begin{document}
    Let $(a_n)_n$ be a positive sequence of real numbers which converges to zero. Let $B = \{b_1,b_2,b_3,...\}$ be a countable subset of $[0,1]$. Consider the function:
        \begin{equation*}
        \begin{split}
            f(x) = 
            \begin{cases}
                a_n, & x=b_n \\
                0, & x \not\in B.
            \end{cases}
        \end{split}
        \end{equation*}
    Show that $f$ is Riemann integrable on $[0,1]$.
